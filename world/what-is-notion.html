What is Notion? The best productivity app that will organize your life.

Notion is a cross-platform task organizer that allows you to take your personal and work planning to the next level. Whether you're connected to the internet or not, on a desktop or from your cell phone, you'll be able to write down and organize everything you can think of and collaborate with whoever you need.

Maybe you've already been looking for one application to write down ideas, tasks or expenses. Notion allows you to write texts, lists, checkboxes or bullets, and order this information in tables, databases, wikis or entries.

For added order, Notion allows you to add navigation filters and find everything quickly. In addition, you can add pages and subpages and organize them at will, like folders on your computer.

How does Notion work?

To create a Notion account, you just need to link an email or Google account. This will allow that, regardless of the device with which you connect, your information can be synchronized.

This is an alternative that the Notion website imitated from many other platforms and apps, not only to keep the different accounts you have synchronized, but also to speed up the registrations that are often very extensive and tedious.

The tool works great on the web, but you can also download Notion for Windows, Mac, Android, and iOS. Even if you're offline, the program allows you to continue editing from your laptop, plus it syncs new information when you're back online.

You already know what Notion is and what it is for. Read on for more details on Notion's pricing and features.
